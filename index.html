SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple choclate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

EQUIPMENT

  • Cake tin or muffin tray
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer
  • Cooling rack
  • Fork
  • Weighing scales
  • Smaller bowls, to hold ingredients

INGREDIENTS

Once you have all your equipment ready its time to weigh the ingredients, so let's get started!

  • 160g -- self-rising flour
  • 160g -- room temp butter
  • 160g -- sugar
  • tbsp -- cocoa
  • 3 -- large eggs

MIX

Preheat the oven to 180oC and while that's heating up beat the butter smooth, add the sugar and cocoa and beat until smooth consistency. Add the flour bit by bit, combining to the mix. Next add the adds to a secondary bowl and beat for a minute or two by hand. Add the eggs to the main mixture and combine all.

IN THE OVEN

Put the mix into a greased cake tin and then straight into the oven for 35 mins or until golden brown.

YOU CAN STYLE THE CAKE ANY WAY YOU WANT
